Cheri Croffle

Cheri Croffle is a small business made for our entrepreneurship event, selling delicious and affordable croffles to senior high and college students. Croffles combine croissants and waffles for a crispy yet soft treat. Our goal is to offer a tasty, budget-friendly snack students can enjoy while exploring the expo booths.

Name of Company

Cheri Croffle combines “Cheri” (French for “beloved”) and “Croffle” (croissant + waffle), reflecting our goal to offer tasty, affordable snacks students will love. Our vision is to be students’ top snack choice by delivering quality, budget-friendly croffles. We value Quality, Affordability, and Passion to create joyful, crave-worthy treats at student-friendly prices.

Vision Statement

To be the go-to snack choice for students,known for our quality, affordability, and great taste, making every bite more enjoyable.

Mission Statement

To provide delicious, affordable, and satisfying croffles to students, giving them a tasty treat that fits their budget.

Philosophy/Core Values

Good food should be affordable, satisfying, and made to be shared—just like our croffles.

Core Values:

Quality, Affordability, Passion
